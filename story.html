Are licensed sets bigger?

Each arc is either a theme group or a piece-size tier. Chord width = how many sets belong to that combination. The diagram reveals that Licensed sets skew surprisingly small — collector Technic and Creator sets hold the piece-count crown. Hover any arc or chord for details.
